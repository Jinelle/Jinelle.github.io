Welcome Traveller

As the name suggests, There and Back is an adventure travel company that knows how to take you off the beaten track, provide the adventure of a lifetime, and get you there and back safely. All of our adventure packages are put together and escorted by highly competent local staff, who will give you an authentic adventure experience. Our tours are not for the armchair traveller or for the unadventurous. They will take you into the heart of the country and bring you back with a new view of the world.

We are proudly based in New Zealand, but we can book you on any of our adventure packages from anywhere in the world. Why not check out our tour packages? We offer tours in Africa, South America, Asia and New Zealand. You will find one that suits your taste for adventure and your budget.

Not sure whether There and Back adventure tourism is for you? Check out the feedback from our satisfied clients. Many have come back for more.

Travel with There and Back, and come back delighted.
